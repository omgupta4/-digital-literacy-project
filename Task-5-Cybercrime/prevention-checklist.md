# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## ✅ General Safety Tips
- Always use strong and different passwords for your accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible, as this will provide a higher level of security.
- Do not click on suspicious links or open any attachments sent in unknown emails or messages.
- Before logging in, always check if the URL is original.
- Try to avoid using public Wi-Fi networks for activities.
- Always keep your phone, laptop, and apps updated in order to stay safe from new security risks.

## 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never disclose your UPI PIN, OTP, or any other bank details, no matter who asks for them.
- Do not accept any payment requests from unknown people on Google Pay, PhonePe, or other similar apps.
- Always double-check the receiver's name before making any transactions.
- Be careful while using QR codes, as they can sometimes deceive you.

## 🚨 In Case of Cybercrime
- If you think something is suspicious, report it right away on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- You can also call the Cyber Crime Helpline on 1930 in case of any emergency.
- Also, inform your bank in case of any suspicion of fraud.
