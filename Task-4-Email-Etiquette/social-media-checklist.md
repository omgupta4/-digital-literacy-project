# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional profile and bio
- Think before posting any content
- Respect others’ opinions and privacy
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
- Share useful and informative content

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in online arguments or harassment
- Avoid clicking suspicious links
- Do not spread misinformation or fake news
